Method Podcast, Episode 1

Prototyping, tools, and designers who code: A conversation with Jay Wong of Project Fi

Hosted by Interaction Designer Aidan Simpson, the Method podcast profiles designers at Google, giving listeners an inside look at their journeys and design choices. In the first episode, Aidan interviews UX Design Lead Jay Wong about her experience working as a designer with a computer science degree, some of the design decisions and challenges
